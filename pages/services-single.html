Standard Questions

Who is the Poomo template designed for?

Poomo is crafted specifically for digital agencies, creative studios, freelancers, marketing teams, and startups. Whether you're building your first online presence or redesigning an existing one, Poomo offers the flexibility and features needed to showcase your services, projects, and brand professionally.
Is the Poomo template responsive and mobile-friendly?

Yes, Poomo is fully responsive and designed to provide a seamless browsing experience across all devices. Whether your visitors are using a desktop, tablet, or smartphone, the layout, content, and visuals will automatically adjust to fit the screen perfectly.
Can I customize the Poomo template to match my brand?

Absolutely. Poomo is built with customization in mind. You can easily change the colors, fonts, images, and layout structure to reflect your brand identity. No coding knowledge is required — everything can be done visually through the platform’s editor.
Does the template include sections like a portfolio or blog?

Yes, Poomo comes with built-in sections for both a portfolio and a blog. The portfolio allows you to display your projects in a clean and modern format, while the blog section is great for publishing news, case studies, or insights to engage your audience and improve SEO.
Is support available after purchasing the template?

Yes, purchasing the Poomo template includes dedicated support. If you run into any issues or have questions about customization, setup, or functionality, our support team is ready to assist you to ensure everything runs smoothly.
